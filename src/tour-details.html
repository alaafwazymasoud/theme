About this tour

Immerse yourself in the magic of Paris with this exclusive 5-day getaway. Designed for couples and romance seekers, this tour takes you beyond the Eiffel Tower to discover hidden gems, charming cafes, and world-class art. Enjoy a private sunset cruise on the Seine, a guided tour of the Louvre after hours, and an intimate wine tasting session in Montmartre.

Accommodations are provided in a boutique hotel in the heart of Le Marais, giving you the perfect base to explore the city's cobblestone streets and historic architecture.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Welcome Dinner

Arrive at CDG Airport where a private car will take you to your hotel. Evening welcome dinner at a classic Parisian bistro.

Day 2: The Icons of Paris

Skip-the-line access to the Eiffel Tower and a guided walking tour of the Champs-Élysées. Afternoon tea at Ladurée.

Day 3: Art & Culture

Private tour of the Louvre Museum. Explore the artistic streets of Montmartre in the afternoon.

Day 4: Versailles Day Trip

A trip to the magnificent Palace of Versailles to see the Hall of Mirrors and the royal gardens.

Day 5: Departure

Breakfast at the hotel and private transfer to the airport for your flight home.
