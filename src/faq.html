Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tours and services.

Booking is easy! Simply navigate to the tour page you are interested in, select your preferred date and number of guests, and click the "Book Now" button. Follow the prompts to complete your reservation.

We offer full refunds for cancellations made 60 days or more before departure. For cancellations made between 30 and 59 days, we offer a 50% refund. Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds for cancellations made less than 30 days before the tour.

Generally, international flights are not included in our tour packages to give you the flexibility to choose your preferred airline and departure city. However, domestic flights within the tour destination may be included as specified in the itinerary.

Yes, we highly recommend that all travelers purchase comprehensive travel insurance to cover unexpected cancellations, medical emergencies, and other unforeseen events.

Many of our tours are family-friendly, but some may have age restrictions due to the nature of the activities. Please check the specific details on the tour page or contact us for more information.
